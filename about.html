About me

I've been a professional QA for over tens years and doing QA Volunteer work for longer. I got my toes wet with Popcap Games and fell in love with testing. I keep reading QA blogs and they all seem to be either way below me or way above me. Thus to fill in the middle ground, I've started a blog with encouragement from my advocate.
